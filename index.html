Frequently asked questions

Quick answers to common questions about repairs, insurance, and timing.

Do you work with my insurance company?

Yes. We work with all major insurance companies and can help you understand your estimate and next steps.

Can I bring my car even if the other driver was at fault?

Yes. You can choose the repair shop. We can coordinate with the responsible insurance company as needed.

How long will repairs take?

Timing depends on the damage and parts availability, but we will give you an estimated timeline after the inspection and keep you updated.
